Professional Creative Environment

Industry Intern at Autonomy Music Group

During my time at Autonomy Music Group in London, I immersed myself in the vibrant UK music scene, capturing authentic moments that helped artists connect with their audiences. This experience transformed my understanding of music industry branding and content creation, allowing me to develop skills that bridge creative storytelling with strategic marketing.

As a Project Manager Assistant, I balanced office responsibilities with creative fieldwork, attending late-night shows and industry events while supporting the team's administrative needs. My position involved collaborating directly with artists to develop their visual identities and create content that resonated with their unique sounds. This dual role gave me invaluable insights into both the business and creative sides of artist management.

The London music scene provided a dynamic backdrop for my work, with its rich diversity of genres and passionate community of creators. Autonomy's approach to artist development emphasized authenticity and innovation, encouraging artists to express their unique voices while building sustainable careers. I quickly adapted to the rhythm of balancing daytime strategy sessions with evening performances, capturing content that told compelling stories about the artists and their journeys.
