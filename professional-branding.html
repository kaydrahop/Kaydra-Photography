Professional Branding

Elevated branding photography and visual identity for professionals. From authors to executives, creating images that communicate authority and personality.

Hairstylist Grace Saltmarsh

Grace came to me wanting to establish a visual identity that authentically represented her hairdressing services specializing in color, editorial styling, and cosmetology. After years in business, she had experienced uncomfortable homophobic clients and wanted imagery that clearly signaled her proudly queer identity while showcasing her young, spunky personality. We crafted an inclusive shoot blending her professional work with personal authenticity - vibrant colors, bold styling, and confident energy. Our second shoot focused on availability branding for appointments plus positioned her for future hair product collaborations. Fun outtakes from the second shoot are in the full gallery.

Noelle Perry at SportsIllustrtated

I brought Noelle's WWE author energy into the studio to create an identity showcasing her as both professional author and passionate wrestling journalist. She wanted imagery that positioned her authority across LinkedIn, Sports Illustrated, and social media while celebrating her WWE fandom. We brought WWE trading cards, action figures, and the heavyweight championship belt into the studio, composing shots that blended professional headshots with authentic wrestling passion. The result was a versatile photo set communicating her dual identity as credentialed journalist and dedicated fan - perfect for bylines, profiles, and personal branding.
