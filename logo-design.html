JunePeak Marketing Project

Full rebrand including logo, website identity, and marketing collateral for digital agency.

Crafting a Modern Identity

This project was a collaboration with Northeastern University's Brand Management class, where I had the opportunity to work on a professional-level brand refresh for JunePeak. The challenge was to modernize their visual identity while maintaining their core values and market position.

JunePeak's goal was to elevate their brand presence, modernize their visual elements, and create a cohesive brand kit that would help them stand out in the competitive digital marketing space. They wanted to revolutionize digital marketing through personalized strategies, and their visual identity needed to reflect this innovative approach.

Deliverables:

  • A sleek, modernized logo inspired by nature and growth, chosen as the top design by survey participants.
  • A cohesive set of branding materials including fonts, colors, and imagery for consistent application across digital assets.
  • Updated messaging to align with JunePeak's vision of revolutionizing digital marketing through personalized strategies.

1. Research & Insights

  • Conducted surveys and interviews with 52 respondents to understand consumer behavior, brand perception, and market segmentation.
  • Tested multiple logo designs to identify the one that resonated most with the target audience, with 67% of respondents selecting the final design.

2. Brand Strategy Development

  • Refined JunePeak's mission statement to emphasize their role in helping businesses grow through tailored digital solutions.
  • Established a clear brand positioning strategy that differentiated them from competitors in the digital marketing space.

3. Creative Execution

  • Designed a modern, nature-inspired logo symbolizing growth, connection, and innovation—core values of JunePeak.
  • Developed a cohesive brand kit including typefaces, color palettes, and customized icons for consistent use across all customer touchpoints.
